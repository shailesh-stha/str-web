We are str.ucture

...an architecture and engineering firm based in Stuttgart. As a company, we are committed to creating a sustainable and CO₂-neutral built environment. Our focus is on a lightweight construction approach that emphasizes the minimization of material consumption and the use of natural and sustainable materials. Digital tools and networked planning technologies help us to develop innovative and sustainable solutions with our partners in interdisciplinary teams. Simulation techniques such as flow or urban climate simulation enable us to understand and minimize the impact of buildings on the microclimate in cities. We also rely on data-based planning and artificial intelligence methods. We work in numerous collaborations with companies and experts as well as in the context of national and international research programs to integrate state-of-the-art developments into planning processes and develop them further. We are your partner for digital transformation in the field of urban climate and are at your side to strengthen urban resilience and effectively design climate adaptation measures.

Our Services at glance:

  1. Digital Urban Climate Model (Palm4U): Creation of a digital urban climate model of your city based on your available data.
  2. Microclimate Simulation data: Provide urban microclimate simulation data at specific times of the day and night. Conduct a specialized assessment of this data in critical target areas, considering development structures, environmental factors, and microclimate parameters.
  3. Digital Data Processing: Digitization and preparation of simulation data into user-friendly web applications and information pages for the public.
  4. Integration in GIS Systems: Seamless integration of simulation data into local GIS (Geographic Information Systems) for optimal use and management of the information.
  5. Visualization of Solutions: Provide location-specific solutions and scenario analysis to provide simple applications available to the public and increase their awareness.
  6. Cost-Benefit Assessment: Evidence-based evaluation of resilience and climate protection measures, serving as a tool for informed infrastructure planning, monitoring, and reporting in decision-making processes.
  7. Utilizing Weather Forecast Models: Incorporating global and local climate models and services to enhance the prediction, monitoring, and reporting of climate events.
